Ethan Lan (b. 2000) is a Taiwanese American visual artist specializing in photography and painting. Born to immigrant parents, Ethan’s work delves into the complexities of cultural background, family ties, trauma, and justice. For him, these themes serve as a profound means of self-discovery and “becoming more myself.”

Through a lens of personal and collective experience, Ethan explores the intersection of identity, resilience, and organizing, creating work that is both intimate and socially resonant. His art invites viewers to reflect on the shared struggles and triumphs that shape our humanity.
